Bullying Canada

Executive Summary

On this UI redesign for non-profit organization Bullying Canada, I used my collaboration with two other team members to develop a solution to empower even more bullied youth to find help. I used my aptitude for empathy in combination with user-centred research, ideation, design and prototype skills to find a solution that would enable the user to find immediate resources to relief their distress. Bullying Canada is Canada’s premier-and first youth-created-anti-bullying charity. It has served hundreds of thousands of youth across the country by, amongst other initiatives, providing individual support, extensive resources, case management, scholarships, and presentations to schools and organizations. Their website is goldmine of information, however it’s poor in its delivery. The content was carefully scrutinized, restructure and new materials were added crafted for the user.
